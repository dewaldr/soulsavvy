Services

Counselling is based on eclectic views of humankind employing Positive Psychology, Mindfulness and short-term Solution Focused therapeutic techniques among others to target thought-provoking content. This allows for finding the right approach and technique to fit the client and situation. Individual and Group Counselling sessions are available to Adults, Adolescents and Children. Our team of counsellors are insightful, sensitive and competent first line professional psychologists. The primary purpose of counselling is to create trusting relationships in a safe environment where the client is assured of a non-judgemental ear and unconditional positive regard. The advantages of attending a counselling session at this practice is that the medical aid rates offered by professionals are affordable, and in some instances, charges are negotiable and raised on a sliding scale. I am a member of a National Counselling Network of psychologists who counsel countrywide both inhouse and use internet-based communication via Zoom to counsel clients who are unable to attend face to face sessions at the practices.

Relationship Therapeutic Approaches

Relationship Counselling includes couples, pre-post marital and family and parenting conflict management.Systemic Therapy is often used for family counselling and enables the identification of dysfunctional patterns of communication and other behaviours across family members.Reality Therapy helps clients to establish greater control over their lives while enhancing the ability to build meaningful and effective relationships. It is a present-day, non-symptom-focused approach in which the counsellor takes on a friendly, positive, and non-judgmental stance.
